🏛️ Problem 1: The Temple Entrance

After weeks of relentless exploration, you finally stand before the entrance of an ANCIENT TEMPLE.

A massive stone door blocks your path. At its center glows a numeric lock, waiting for a 6-digit password.

Etched into the surface are the words:

“The answer lies among the stones. Only one follows the true pattern.”
Scattered across the ground are countless mystical stones, each engraved with a 6-digit number. A record of all these numbers has been provided to you.

Somewhere within these numbers lies the key.

🔍 The Rules of the Lock

A number can unlock the temple only if it satisfies all of the following:

Pattern of the Temple:
Each digit of the number must follow the sacred sequence:

O O O E E O

Where:
O represents an odd digit
E represents an even digit
The pattern is applied from left to right.

Example:
579223 →
5 (odd), 7 (odd), 9 (odd), 2 (even), 2 (even), 3 (odd) → ✅ follows pattern
582613 →
5 (odd), 8 (even ❌ expected odd) → ❌ breaks pattern

Balance of the Digits
The digit itself must also be divisible by 13.

Among all the numbers provided, only one number satisfies both the pattern and the balance.
🧠 Your Task

Find the number that unlocks the temple.
If no such number exists, the temple remains sealed.
The silence of the temple awaits your answer.
