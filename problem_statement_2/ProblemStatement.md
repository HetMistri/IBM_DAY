🏛️ Problem 2: The Ciphered Path

As the ancient temple door opens, a dim corridor leads you into a hidden chamber.

The air is still. The walls are covered in faded inscriptions.
At the center lies a glowing stone tablet.

A message slowly appears:
“The path is hidden in words. Decode them, and the temple shall guide your steps.”

Beneath it, an 8 × 8 grid of alphabets emerges.
You feel it — the grid is not meant to be searched.
It is meant to be followed.
🔍 The Cipher
You are given an encoded string:

ngr%htqqfgtwfyj%nhy

To reveal the true message:
Shift each character’s ascii value backward by 5
🧠 The Hidden Instructions
The decoded string is not just a message.
It is a sequence of movements.
Each character corresponds to a direction:
Characters from A to F → move UP
Characters from G to L → move DWN
Characters from M to R → move LEFT
Characters from S to Z → move RIGHT
Ignore any non-alphabet characters.

🎯 Your Task
Decode the given string
Convert each character into its corresponding movement
Output the full sequence of movements in order
